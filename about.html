Skillman St. is a new creative and stylish fashion brand that started in Bedford Stuyvesant, Brooklyn. The brand was created when I, while attending St. Francis College, decided to follow my dream of working in fashion. Leaving college to focus on this goal, I found inspiration in the people, energy, and culture of BedStuy. Skillman St. became known locally for designs that let people show off their unique style. Every piece is inspired by the streets and community of Brooklyn, blending creativity and everyday life. From the start, the goal has been to make clothes that tell a story and connect with real people. Today, Skillman St. is growing as a brand in the fashion world. It stays true to its Brooklyn roots while encouraging others to go after their dreams. Skillman St. isn’t just about clothes—it’s about taking chances and turning ideas into reality.
