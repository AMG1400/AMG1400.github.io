Skillman St. is a bold, creative fashion brand born in the heart of Bedford Stuyvesant, Brooklyn. The journey began while I was attending St. Francis College, driven by a deep passion for fashion. I made the life changing decision to leave school and chase my dream, drawing endless inspiration from the people, energy, and culture of BedStuy. What started as a local project quickly gained attention for its unique designs that let individuals express their personal style. Every piece reflects the spirit of Brooklyn raw, real, and full of life. From day one, Skillman St. has been about more than just fashion it’s about storytelling, community, and staying true to who you are. As the brand continues to grow, it remains rooted in Brooklyn’s streets while inspiring others to follow their own path. Skillman St. isn’t just clothing it’s a movement built on creativity, courage, and turning vision into reality.
